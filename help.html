How to play

Purpose of the game

The goal of the game is to hit the pig in the least number of attempts

Controls

  • Press W or ↑ to move catapult forward
  • Press S or ↓ to move catapult forward
  • Press D or → to move catapult forward
  • Press A or ← to move catapult forward
  • Press Spacebar to shoot ammo
  • Use sliders "Mass" and "Force" for the best shot
